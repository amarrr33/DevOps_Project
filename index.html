Low Compute Multimodal Lecture Intelligence System

A lightweight AI-driven system designed to analyze, summarize, and enhance lecture content using audio, visual, and textual modalities while operating efficiently on low-compute environments.

Core Objectives

Low Compute Design

Optimized models and pipelines that work efficiently on CPUs and edge devices without requiring high-end GPUs.

Multimodal Understanding

Processes audio (speech), visuals (slides/board), and text to generate unified lecture insights.

Educational Intelligence

Automatically extracts key topics, summaries, and important timestamps from lectures.

System Components

Speech-to-Text Module

Lightweight ASR models convert lecture audio into structured transcripts.

Visual Frame Analyzer

Keyframe extraction from lecture videos to detect slide changes and board content.

Text Intelligence Engine

Summarization, keyword extraction, and concept mapping using efficient NLP models.

Applications

Smart Lecture Notes

Auto-generated concise notes aligned with lecture timelines.

Accessible Education

Helps students with limited devices or connectivity access intelligent learning tools.

Academic Analytics

Insights into lecture pacing, topic coverage, and content density.
